Instructions

From the Home screen you have an option to Play and an option for Nightlight. In the Play mode, each touch of the screen creates a random image that then floats across the screen before fading away. Maintaining contact with the screen produces more of the same image. In the Nightlight screen, images do not vanish from the screen and your device will not shut off due to lack of interaction with the app. It is recommended that you leave your device plugged in if you do not want your battery drained or set a timer through the Settings screen to turn off your app and preserve your battery.
